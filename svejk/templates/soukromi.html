What we collect

For the newsletter we only need your e-mail address. On the website we track basic visit statistics, such as how many people opened a page, which articles they read, and where they came from. We do not need your name, address, or payment card.

Why we use it

We use your e-mail to send new editions of the Chamber diary. Analytics helps us see whether anyone reads the site and what interests readers. It is not used to sell ads or market third-party products. No pot offers. No surprises.

Where data is stored

We do not store e-mails directly on this site. Sending is handled for us by Ecomail. Visit statistics are measured by Google Analytics; we only see aggregate summaries.

Cookies

We use Google Analytics only with your consent. .

Newsletter and retention

Every e-mail includes an unsubscribe link. After you unsubscribe, we remove your address from the mailing list.

Your rights

You can ask anytime what data we hold about you or request deletion. For analytics you can change your cookie choice on this page or delete cookies in your browser.

Voluntary contributions

Payments for beer, rum, or the regular supporter tier are processed by Stripe. You enter card details directly on Stripe pages and this site does not store them.
